Our Services

7 Reasons to Hire Us

Price Competitive

A sound business structure and careful asset management have allowed us to keep prices at affordable levels without compromise on quality. We provide the best of professional artistes, music solutions and support, all within your budget.

In-house Sound Systems

We're guilty of splurging a lifetime's worth of savings on one too many fantastic top-of-the-line BOSE F-1 and L1-Series speakers! So if you need affordable sound systems support for our bands, ask about our exclusive BOSE sound packages. You'll be glad you did.

Musical Instruments

We realize the hassle of having our clients pay little extras here and there on instrument rental above the band prices. So we decided to include the rental of music instruments to our clients free-of-charge.

This potentially saves our clients estimated rental costs of $100 for a digital piano, and $200 for a drum-set.

Reliability

Musical Instruments - we always have that extra set on standby.​

Sound Systems - we also always have that extra set on standby.

Musicians - our team consists of over 180 musicians.

These should give you peace of mind about anything going wrong as we have it covered.

Impression Counts

Impressions are of foremost importance. All our bands and musicians perform in coordinated attires, and never in a mess of colors. Our wardrobe currently holds over a few hundred colored ties, blazers and themed attire of sorts, just so that we look our best at performances.

Unique Concept Bands

Other than our extensive list of boutique bands, we create ambience and atmospheres of movie-worthy moments. Swinging 20's-40's. Traditional Asian. Caribbean Moods. All of these above providing the best wedding bands.

Wide Repertoire Capability

English & Mandarin Pop, Jazz, Shanghai Classics, Evergreens, Modern Jazz, Funk, Latin Bossa Nova, Swing, Improvisations – our team of musicians can do all of these at professional levels. Let us know your choice of music and we will perform, usually beyond expectations.
