The Wedding Music Company

Singapore’s Largest Team of Wedding Vocalists

The Wedding Music Company is a subsidiary of High Notes – Singapore’s forefront music provider of live music entertainment. Previously known as Love Notes, TWMC aims to provide its clients with not only the best of live music, but also a myriad of possibilities, with the largest vocalist choice, coupled with the finest of musicians in Singapore.

Behind TWMC is the same production team which has managed over two thousand events and weddings the past seven years, putting together entertainment for some of the most elaborate weddings and working with celebrities such as Lee Hom, Gigi Leung, Angelica Lee, Charlie Yeung and Vanness Wu, among others.

It is our vision to be the most service-focused and quality-delivering live music provider in Singapore, with a mission to provide reliable, innovative and outstanding live entertainment to all our clients.

Pick The Perfect wedding Band

High Notes, founded in 2008 by three musicians to bring music to elderly and children homes, has since provided live music for over two thousand events and weddings both locally and internationally. Specializing in jazz and commercial live music, the team puts creative innovation at its forefront, while delivering outstanding operational and service levels to bring the best of live music to Singapore.
