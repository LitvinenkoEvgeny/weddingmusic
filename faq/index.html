Frequently Asked Questions

Why should we choose TWMC?

Experience the highest levels of live entertainment coupled with most excellent service, all at the most affordable prices. And we achieve this with a very attentive full time team who takes great pride in every show. Plus, we have been doing this for seven years.

Do we really need a band?

The right live band will set the perfect tone, be sensitive to the mood of your guests, and help everyone have a great time. This said, you would need a band with the experience to craft a desirable song-list that would progress throughout the evening; a sound engineer that ensures the right mix and volume levels for your guests, and a manager that knows how to put it all together, i.e. music for your march-in, first-dish presentation, sabrage ceremony, and the little moments to fit the music in. This is why you need a right team, and a right band.

How do I choose the right live band for my wedding?

TWMC is the first and only site that lets you choose from over fifty wedding vocalists, with genre and language filters, and to fix a band together according to your preference of size and budget. Click here to pick the perfect wedding band for your upcoming wedding!

Where do we fit your band into our programme?

Little to worry about this part as we will fit the band sets into your programme. Better still, should you need any help at all fixing together a banquet schedule, get in touch with us and we will guide you along with putting together a schedule suited to you. With experience of over a thousand weddings, we know what can be done, and all that should be done.

What songs will your band play?

Upon receiving your enquiry with your vocalist choices, we will send you their song-lists. You could either choose songs from this list, or have us put together a list of the vocalist’s best performed tunes.

Can your band play my favourite songs?​

Our musicians will definitely be able to perform your favorite songs. Should the songs be not in their list, we will proceed to check in with them. In a case where the vocalist is not able to, we will inform you in advance and work with you to come up with a song-list of your choice way before your big day.

Can I sing a song with your band to surprise my partner?

Please do! There is nothing we love better than have the limelight on you. We could either work out rehearsals before your big day or plan to sneak you out before the celebrations begin to rehearse with our band. Our experienced musicians will be most glad to guide and accompany you.

Do you take dedications?​

Yes we do! Upon request, we also provide complimentary dedication slips for your guests to write well wishes and special messages on. We will then collect these momentos for your keeps after the wedding.

Who provides all the equipment?

All necessary musical instruments are complimentary upon engagement of our bands. Plus, we also provide top-of-the-line sound systems at the most competitive prices.

Should you already have your own sound systems provider, we will furnish you with a list of required equipment and work closely with your venue and provider on the day.

You mean we really don’t have to pay for rental of the digital piano, drums, et al?

All complimentary. Plus, we always have extra sets in our own fleet of lorries. This means any malfunction (none to date!) can be replaced immediately. So include peace of mind into the complimentary part of it too.

What will it cost us?

Our rates start from as low as $520 for a band, and up to $2,500 for a 11-piece Great Gatsby-style big band. Unbelievable? Contact us for our latest rate-cards, you’ll be glad you did.

Any other additional costs? GST?

Guaranteed nett prices. Rest assured that we are upfront with everything you need to know. We also list details of each package in our quotations; everything else that we need, we will bring along. No surprises.

How far in advance must we book your band?

We always tell our clients that the sooner we get confirmation, the more likely that your choice of vocalist is available.

We have encountered occasions where confirmations come in late and choice vocalists have already been booked by others, and we don’t like to disappoint.

Do you provide professional emcee services?

Most of our vocalists double up eloquently as wedding emcees at a nominal fee.

We do also provide professional wedding emcees who would entertain with segments beyond the regular wedding script. Check in with us on our recommendations to fit the right emcee personality to your wedding day ideals.

OK, we’re sold. So what do we do now?

It’s pretty easy to get started. Go here to put together your preferred wedding band. Our web-selection tool would help much with your choice of a wedding band with genre, band size, budget, and other consideration points.

Submit your enquiry via the 3-step pick-a-wedding-band system and we will get back to you within one working day (most often within a few hours).

Help, we still have questions!

Contact us via email at enquiry@weddingmusic.com.sg, contact form or call us at 66356622. It is always a pleasure to be of any help at all.

Do you also perform for events other than weddings?

Yes of course we do. In fact, we cannot think of any kinds of events which we have not been engaged to perform for. Just email us at enquiry@weddingmusic.com.sg, contact form or call us at 66356622 to get in touch, and you will experience the same level of excellent service and operational team to assist you on your event.
