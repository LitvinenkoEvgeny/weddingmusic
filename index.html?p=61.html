Summertimes Hotshots Jazz Quartet

Summertimes Hotshots is the baby outfit of the Summertimes bands. Reliving jazz swing standards of the 20s-40s, and often with a vocalist too, this band has been swinging hard at high profile events such as the MediaCorp Festival, CNA Luminary Awards and the Singapore Corporate Awards, recreating the  golden era of swing very much to the delight of audiences. They also perform all other genres of jazz like the Blues & Bossa Nova.

Los Pintos – Singapore’s Best Strollers

Los Pintos has been entertaining guests in Singapore for close to a decade. It’s repertoire spans across familiar Chinese and English oldies and 80s pop, and often whipping up Hokkien, Malay and even Japanese favorites to the delight of many. Their spry style of playing while they stroll is pure entertainment!

Shanghai Swingers

Been to 和平饭店 (The Peace Hotel)  in Shanghai and you would have heard of the exotic atmosphere created by the Old Jazz Band. Reflecting the western-influenced days of glamorous cheong sums and rustic splendor, the Shanghai Swingers will keep audiences enchanted and dancers swinging all night thorugh, with lovely chanteuses singing a delightful mix of both Chinese and English jazz standards.
